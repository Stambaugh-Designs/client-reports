Executive Summary

Radiant Heart's online presence is building strong momentum. In its first 30 days of tracked performance, the site is already ranking for 665 organic keywords — with 10 in the top 3 positions and 117 on page one of Google. For a local pet cremation service in Bellingham, this is an excellent foundation.

The SEO work by Stambaugh Designs is clearly paying off. Google Search Console shows the site pulling in 279 clicks from 14,624 impressions, with an average position of 9.2 — meaning the site is, on average, appearing on page one. Brand awareness is growing rapidly: branded searches like "radiant heart" and "radiant heart bellingham" are ranking #1–2 with exceptional 36–41% click-through rates, well above industry benchmarks.

Perhaps most encouraging is the +266 new keywords discovered this period with a strong net upward trend (244 keywords improving vs. 136 declining). The site's topical footprint is expanding into valuable commercial queries like "pet cremation near me" (position 2.3), "water cremation pets" (position 4.9), and product-specific terms for urns and memorial stones.

Referral partnerships with local veterinary clinics — including Madrona Vet, Kulshan Vet, and Bell Mobile Vet Care — are proving especially valuable, with visitors from these sources showing 0–20% bounce rates and high engagement. This is a strong channel worth expanding.

There are two quick-win technical fixes that Stambaugh Designs has identified and will be addressing: two product category pages returning 404 errors (currently the site's highest-traffic URLs) and a www/non-www consolidation that will unify ranking signals. Once resolved, we expect a meaningful lift in both traffic and rankings heading into next month's report.

Bottom line: Radiant Heart is well-positioned for continued organic growth. The keyword trajectory, brand visibility, and local search presence all point in the right direction — and with the technical fixes on deck, next month should show even stronger numbers.

Key Insights & Recommendations
🚨

Fix 404 Errors on Product Category Pages

/product-category/glass-jewelry-keychains/ (1,328 views) and /product-category/pet-urns/ (960 views) are returning "Page Not Found" — yet they're the two most-visited URLs in Rybbit. These are likely indexed in Google and sending organic visitors to dead ends. Set up 301 redirects to the correct pages immediately.

🔗

Consolidate WWW vs Non-WWW

The homepage is splitting GSC data between www (87 clicks, pos 4.1) and non-www (51 clicks, pos 11.7). Implementing a canonical redirect will unify ranking signals and should improve the non-www variant's position significantly.

🤖

Investigate Bot Traffic (67% of Sessions)

2,633 sessions from Singapore with 99.4% bounce rate is almost certainly automated traffic. Consider adding bot filtering in Rybbit or implementing rate limiting from non-target geographies to get cleaner analytics data.

📝

Optimize High-Impression, Low-Click Product Pages

"Paw Print Memorial Stones" has 1,368 impressions but only 10 clicks (0.7% CTR at pos 10.7). "Custom Ceramic Cat Urn" has 1,075 impressions at 0.5% CTR. Rewriting title tags and meta descriptions on these product pages to be more compelling could double or triple click-through rates.

⭐

Strong Local Brand Presence

Brand queries ("radiant heart", "radiant heart bellingham") rank #1–2 and drive the majority of search clicks with 36–41% CTR. This indicates growing local brand awareness and trust.

📈

Keyword Growth is Strong

266 new keywords discovered this period with a net positive movement (244 up vs 136 down). The site's topical footprint is expanding well, particularly in product-related and "near me" queries.

🏥

Vet Referral Partners Are Converting Exceptionally Well

Traffic from riversrelease.com (0%), madronavet.com (20%), bellmobilevetcare.com (0%), and kulshanvet.com (0%) all show very low bounce rates — these visitors are highly engaged and likely converting. Consider expanding vet partnership outreach to other Bellingham-area clinics.
